# Suggested Sections by Research Type

These are section menus, not mandatory templates. Pick what fits. Skip what doesn't. Add sections not listed here when the topic calls for it.

Only the **header block** (title, date, status, author) is required for every document.

---

## Technical Investigation

Researching a technical approach, library, pattern, or architecture question.

- **Problem Statement / Current State** — what exists now and why it's insufficient
- **Goals** — what success looks like
- **Non-Goals** — what's explicitly out of scope (prevents scope creep)
- **Options / Approaches** — enumerate alternatives with tradeoffs
- **Recommendation** — which option and why
- **Open Questions** — unresolved items that need input
- **Effort Estimate** — rough sizing if useful

## Architecture Decision

Proposing or documenting a system design choice.

- **Context** — forces and constraints driving the decision
- **Decision** — what was decided
- **Consequences** — tradeoffs accepted, what changes, what doesn't
- **Alternatives Considered** — other options evaluated and why they were rejected

## Vendor Evaluation

Comparing products, services, or tools.

- **Requirements** — what we need and weighted priorities
- **Market Overview** — landscape summary
- **Vendor Comparison** — structured comparison (tables work well)
- **Recommendation** — which vendor and why
- **Pricing** — cost summary or range
- **Next Steps** — trial, procurement, integration plan

## Product / Sales Research

Customer research, market analysis, or sales preparation.

- **Executive Summary** — key findings upfront
- **Company / Market Overview** — background
- **Fit Analysis** — how it connects to our product
- **Key Contacts** — people involved
- **Sources** — where the data came from

## Brainstorm / Exploration

Early-stage thinking without a clear structure yet.

- **Context** — what prompted this
- **Ideas / Observations** — the substance
- **Open Questions** — what to investigate next
